Biller Type Presentment & Payment


Note:

Billers Type:



- Presentment: Billers that present an electronic bill (i.e. you will receive the bill electronically through the HDFC Bank Bill Payment service) and accept a payment against the presented bill.



- Payment: Billers that do not present an electronic bill but accept instant or adhoc payment.


- Presentment and Payment: Billers that present an electronic bill as well as accept instant or adhoc payment.



For LIC:

Payment for Monthly & ULIP mode policies is not accepted by LIC under BillPay.



Only E-receipts will be issued to the policy holders by LIC for Bill payments hence valid email id is compulsory.



In Premium Amount - Kindly enter "basic Premium Amount" only, i.e. exclusive of service tax and interest amount(if any).
