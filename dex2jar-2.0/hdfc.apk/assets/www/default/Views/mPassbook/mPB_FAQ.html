FAQs
  • What is mPassbook?

    mPassbook is a newly added feature in your HDFC Mobile Banking app. You can now update your passbook on your mobile phone using this feature in our app.

    mPassbook offers you an easy way to get regular transaction updates of your Savings & Current account , RD and FD summary and retrieve them offline as well.

    mPassbook is currently available for Android & iPhone users only.

  • What is mPassbook PIN/Pattern?

    mPassbook PIN is 4 digit PIN and Pattern is a custom lock pattern set by you to access mPassbook feature on your HDFC Mobile banking app. Once this PIN or Pattern is set by you in registration process, for your next login you need to only enter the PIN or Pattern in the app to view you Passbook.

  • What can I do using mPassbook?

    You can view your Account balance and transactions of Savings Account, Current Account, Recurring Deposit and Fixed Deposit summary.

    Features

    •  Get your account statement anywhere any time.

    •  Facility to tag transactions and add personalized remarks for transactions.

    •  Search and filter to find a transaction.

    •  Offline mode. (Updates available till last Sync for Android)

    •  You can do a lot more with Interactive statement feature in mPassbook. Interactive statement allows you to tag, categorize your transactions and see a break up of your income /expense transactions.

  • Are there any charges for this facility?

    No, this facility is free of cost for all HDFC customers

  • Which all Account transactions can I see on my mPassbook?

    mPassbook feature will enable customers to view their Savings, Current account transactions and RD and FD summary.

  • Will my transaction history be available offline?

    Yes, transaction history will be available in mPassbook app in offline mode also.

  • How can I know if I was successfully registered for mPassbook?

    On successful registration, Bank will send SMS to registered mobile no. "Dear Customer, Congratulations you have successfully registered for mPassbook"

  • I’ve forgotten my mPassbook PIN/Pattern, how can I regenerate it?

    Incase you have forgetton your mPassbook App PIN or Pattern , you will have to follow the same registration process to reset the PIN/pattern. Select the “Forgot PIN/Pattern” option on Login screen to follow the re-registration process.

  • How to change my login PIN/Pattern?

    You can change your login pin or pattern from Settings option, in left side navigation menu. You will see here the option to change your PIN or Pattern.

  • What is Interactive Statement in mPassbook?

    Interactive statement is an added feature in mPassbook. This feature allows you to add notes to transactions, categorize your transactions and view a breakup/chart of your income and expenses.

  • How can I add a note to a Transaction?

    In Interactive statement, click on the transaction you want to tag and you will get an option of Add personalised notes to that transaction.

  • Can I do any financial transaction like Credit Card Payment, Fund transfer etc.?

    No, this app is only a Digital Passbook for you. Kindly use HDFC mobile banking application for making payments & fund transfer.

  • Can I view the complete transaction history from the day of opening my account?

    mPassbook will allow you to view transactional data of last 6 months of your account from the day of registration and first usage of mPassbook. The transactional data will keep getting updated in the app on subsequent usage of mPassbook feature.

  • Will I be able to see my closed/deactive accounts as well?

    No, mPassbook will only show currently active accounts of customer. De-active/dormant accounts will not be shown in mPassbook.

  • I am not able to see all my active accounts, what to do?

    Please try refreshing the application once (use refresh button on top menu) and if the issue still persists, please contact HDFC Bank Customer Care to check the accounts linked with your User ID

  • For which devices is mPassbook supported?

    mPassbook is currently supported for Android phones (OS 2.3 and above)& iPhone(OS 5.1.1 and above).
    * For optimal experience of mPassbook please use Android 4.0 and above.
    * For optimal experience of mPassbook please use ios 7.0 and above.

  • I have downloaded the app but mPassbook app is not working on my phone, what should I do?

    Please contact HDFC Bank customer care number in “Contact us” section.
